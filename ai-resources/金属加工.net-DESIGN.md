## Overview

Professional and trustworthy with an industrial corporate feel. The design conveys reliability and expertise through its structured layout, conservative color palette, and clear typography. The overall density is moderate with generous white space that creates a clean, organized appearance suitable for B2B manufacturing services.

The layout follows a traditional corporate website structure with a fixed-width container, horizontal navigation, and clear content sections. The design uses consistent margins and padding throughout, with a grid-based approach that emphasizes readability and professional presentation.

Uses a 5px base grid with scale: 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 8.

## Colors
- **Corporate Teal** (#0b6678): Header background, primary branding elements
- **Industrial Navy** (#004f83): Secondary blue elements, darker accents
- **Professional Blue** (#0072bd): Links and interactive elements
- **Accent Cyan** (#45b1c7): Highlights and secondary accents
- **White** (#ffffff): Main background, content areas
- **Light Gray Background** (#f1f5f4): Section backgrounds, subtle areas
- **Black** (#000000): Primary text content
- **Dark Gray** (#333333): Secondary text, headings
- **Medium Gray** (#393939): Body text, descriptions
- **Light Gray** (#a9a9a9): Borders, dividers, subtle elements

## Typography
- **Headline Font**: Meiryo
- **Body Font**: Meiryo
- **Label Font**: Meiryo

The typography system relies exclusively on the Meiryo font family, a Japanese system font that provides excellent readability for both Japanese and Latin characters. The weight convention uses primarily medium (500) weight for most text with bold (700) reserved for emphasis. Size relationships follow a clear hierarchy from 12px for small text up to 28px for main headings, with consistent line-height ratios that maintain readability. The uniform font choice creates a cohesive, professional appearance throughout the interface.

## Elevation

The design uses minimal shadows and relies primarily on color contrast and borders to create hierarchy. Depth is achieved through the layered header design and subtle background color variations rather than dramatic shadow effects, maintaining a flat but structured appearance.

## Components
- **Header Navigation**: Blue header with white logo and navigation menu items
- **Hero Banner**: Large background image with overlay text and call-to-action messaging
- **Navigation Breadcrumbs**: Horizontal breadcrumb navigation with separator characters
- **Content Cards**: White background content areas with subtle borders and spacing
- **Contact Information**: Phone number and business hours display in header area

## Do's and Don'ts
- Do use the blue color palette consistently for branding and navigation elements
- Do maintain generous white space between content sections for clarity
- Do use Meiryo font family for all text to ensure consistency
- Don't use bright or saturated colors that conflict with the professional aesthetic
- Do keep border radius consistent at 30px for interactive elements
- Don't overcrowd the layout - maintain the clean, structured appearance
- Do use medium font weight (500) as the default for body text
- Don't use decorative fonts that would compromise the industrial, professional tone
